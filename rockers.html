About the band
The rockers band was formed in the 5th of july 1996 and consisted of only two members, rockland steve and james ershire, and hence the named rockers from the joining of the first letters of their names, rockland and ershire. later in 2002 two more members joined in forming the present Rockers band but still retaining the original name.
